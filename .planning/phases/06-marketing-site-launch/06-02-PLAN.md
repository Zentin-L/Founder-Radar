<task id="2.1">
## Task: Build timeline-driven section choreography

Define timeline segments:
- Segment A: Hero hold + subtle camera drift
- Segment B: Hero to Problem push-through transition
- Segment C: Solution reveal with momentum gauge focus
- Segment D: How-it-Works progressive reveal stack

Each segment defines camera position, lookAt, FOV easing, and section opacity offsets.
</task>

<task id="2.2">
## Task: Implement layered parallax and transitions

Parallax strategy:
- 3 depth layers per section (foreground/intermediate/background)
- independent transform multipliers by depth
- exit transitions overlap next section entry by 12-18% scroll window

Keep transforms GPU-friendly (translate/scale/opacity only).
</task>

<task id="2.3">
## Task: Wire aggressive pacing without motion sickness

Aggressive style constraints:
- faster narrative advancement, but smooth easing and low jerk camera curves
- cap rotation rates and avoid rapid Z-axis swings
- reduced-motion mode switches to minimal camera movement with 2D fades
</task>
